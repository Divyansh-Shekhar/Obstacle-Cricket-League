Single Team is one of the teams playing from the first season. They have one OCL season to their name, the fourth season. Single Team is a different kind of team, as it sets target it never made. They can set humongous or normal or tiny targets to be chased in a specific number of balls with a specific number of wickets. Their highest target has been 100/2 (8.2) (UR vs ST 2023 Alv-Spd, not chased) and 100/10 (10) (MH vs ST 2024 Jien, not chased). Their lowest target has been 6/1 (0.3) (UR vs ST 2024 Jien, not chased).
